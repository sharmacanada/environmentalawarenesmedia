About the Project

Environmental Awareness Through Social Media is a capstone project that aims to empower individuals to live sustainably by leveraging the digital spaces we use every day.

The project has two main goals:

Education & Awareness – By creating visual, easy-to-digest content about recycling, sustainable living, and climate change, we aim to make sustainability accessible to all.

Engagement & Collective Action – Through online challenges, community discussions, and interactive campaigns, we hope to turn awareness into meaningful action.
